### 1. [AirBnB Market Analysis (Seattle)](https://github.com/BIwithBilly/PortfolioProjects/blob/main/Tableau-Projects/Seattle_AirBnB_Dashboard.png)
*Focus: Real Estate Investment & Revenue Strategy*

![Seattle AirBnB Dashboard](https://github.com/BIwithBilly/PortfolioProjects/blob/main/Tableau-Projects/Seattle_AirBnB_Dashboard.png?raw=true)

* **The Problem:** Investors needed to understand the Seattle AirBnB market to determine which property types and locations yield the highest revenue.
* **The Solution:** Developed a comprehensive dashboard analyzing 2016 listing data, focusing on the relationship between bedroom count and price.
* **Key Insights:** Identified peak booking seasons and established that 4-bedroom properties offered the highest ROI potential in specific zip codes.

### 2. [Global CO2 Emissions Tracker](https://github.com/BIwithBilly/PortfolioProjects/blob/main/Tableau-Projects/CO2_Trend_Dashboard.png)
*Focus: Environmental Data Science & Regional Trends*

![CO2 Trend Dashboard](https://github.com/BIwithBilly/PortfolioProjects/blob/main/Tableau-Projects/CO2_Trend_Dashboard.png?raw=true)
![CO2 Emissions by Region](https://github.com/BIwithBilly/PortfolioProjects/blob/main/Tableau-Projects/C02_Emssions_by_Region_Dashboard.png?raw=true)

* **The Problem:** Visualizing a century of global emission data to identify which regions are accelerating or decelerating their carbon footprint.
* **The Solution:** Created a multi-page dashboard using time-series forecasting and geographic mapping to compare regional contributions.
* **Key Tech:** Dual-axis charts, custom color divergence for intensity mapping, and year-over-year growth calculations.
