### 1. [AirBnB Market Analysis (Seattle)](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/billy.lackey/viz/AirBnBproject_17112461877470/Dashboard1)
*Focus: Real Estate Investment & Revenue Strategy*

- **The Objective:** Analyze 2016 AirBnB listing data to identify pricing trends and investment "hot spots" based on bedroom count and location.
- **Key Insights:**
    - **Revenue Drivers:** Established a clear correlation between bedroom count and Average Daily Rate (ADR).
    - **Geospatial Trends:** Visualized zip-code level data to identify high-value neighborhoods for potential hosts.
    - **Seasonality:** Identified peak booking windows to optimize dynamic pricing strategies.
- **Tech Used:** Map layers, calculated fields for pricing metrics, and interactive dashboard actions.

### 2. [Global CO2 Emissions Tracker](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/billy.lackey/viz/My1stDashboard_17112003730700/Dashboard1)
*Focus: Environmental Data Science & Regional Trends*

- **The Objective:** Track historical CO2 output across global regions to identify the largest contributors and the rate of industrial change.
- **Key Insights:**
    - **Regional Comparison:** Visualized the shift in emission leadership from Western nations to developing industrial regions.
    - **Time-Series Analysis:** Created dual-axis charts to compare total emissions against regional growth over time.
- **Tech Used:** Dual-axis charts, choropleth (heat) maps, and regional filtering.
